Privacy Policy

WeebCentral Manga Downloader Chrome Extension

Last updated: December 2024

Overview

This privacy policy explains how the WeebCentral Manga Downloader Chrome extension collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our extension to download manga content from WeebCentral.

Data Collection

This extension collects the following types of data:

  • Website content: Manga images and text content from WeebCentral pages that you choose to download
  • Extension settings: Your preferences for download speed, file naming, and other settings (stored locally)

Data Usage

All collected data is used solely for the extension's core functionality:

  • Detecting manga pages on WeebCentral when you visit them
  • Extracting manga images and text for download
  • Downloading content to your local computer for offline reading
  • Remembering your extension preferences and settings

Data Storage

Important: All data is stored locally on your device. No data is sent to external servers, cloud storage, or third-party services.

  • Downloaded manga files are saved to your computer's default download folder
  • Extension settings are stored in your browser's local storage
  • No data is transmitted over the internet except for downloading manga content from WeebCentral

Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Sell, share, or transfer any user data to third parties
  • Use your data for advertising or marketing purposes
  • Collect personal information like names, emails, or addresses
  • Track your browsing history beyond the current manga page

Permissions

This extension requires the following permissions:

  • activeTab: To detect manga pages and extract content
  • downloads: To download manga images to your computer
  • storage: To save your extension settings
  • scripting: To inject content scripts for page analysis
  • tabs: To manage tabs and open settings

Third-Party Services

The extension only interacts with:

  • WeebCentral: To access manga content for downloading
  • CDN domains: To download manga images (official.lowee.us, lowee.us)

Data Security

Your data security is important to us:

  • All data processing happens locally on your device
  • No sensitive information is transmitted or stored externally
  • Extension settings are stored securely in your browser

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Uninstall the extension at any time (removes all local data)
  • Clear extension data through your browser settings
  • Choose not to download manga content
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
